About Me

Bio

I am a passionate data nerd! I worked with various types of data including genomic, text image, and time series from IMU sensors. During my graduate studies, I conducted research on multi-modal genomic data and applied deep learning and feature extraction techniques to discover biomarkers for different cancer sybtypes. While interning at Atos Syntel, I prototyped solutions based on neural networks to extract text from parcel lables. Currently, I am working at a fast-paced startup analyzing sensor-based data to track user's motion and provide feedback on his/her golf swing.

Skills

Based on the problem at hand, I exploit proper techniques such as supervised vs. unsupervised learning, old-fashion linear regression vs. cutting-edge deep learning. I always pick the appropriate metric such as confusion matrix, precision and recall, R-squared, and MSE to evaluate my models and not get fooled by a high accuracy. On a daily basis I utilize python, pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, matplotlib, jupyter notebook, and gitlab and I have some experience using R, keras, and tensorflow. I'm a fast-learner and I'm always eager to work with new types of data and use my knowledge to push technology to and past its limits.
